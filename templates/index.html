Welcome to Customer Review Insights

Analyze customer reviews with advanced sentiment analysis, language detection, and translation to gain valuable insights.

How it works
  1. Upload your customer reviews in Excel (.xlsx, .xls) or CSV format
  2. Our system detects the language of each review
  3. Non-English reviews are automatically translated to English
  4. Sentiment analysis is performed on all reviews
  5. View comprehensive insights in an interactive dashboard
